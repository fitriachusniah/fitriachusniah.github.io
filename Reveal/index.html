About Us

MALANG is largest regency in East Java - Indonesia. A beautiful place with friendly people and lot of natural wealth abundantly. We commit to become exporter best quality agricultural and traditional craft. Our commitment to trade best quality products, exceptional services and incomparable customer care kept our customer coming back again and again. Now we have exported these products to serve customers in world wide. We never stop improving ang continuing to expand our offerings, based on how we can best serve the worldwide area.

Vision

Be a company with source and blessing in international business development with Indonesian products to offer to the world.

Mission

Our company aims to contribute healthy and beautiful living by Indonesian commodity high value, quality and respect to the major international standards. Promote the development of export and internationalization projects through advice to Indonesian companies. Our goal is to improve the economic standard of community.
